Friends of Cedars Park are committed to the good care and improvement of our park and supporting our community. Here are some of the projects that we have completed:

  • Heritage Lottery Grant – The Friends Group achieved 700 volunteer days over three years to support a heritage grant application by Broxbourne Borough Council, and the park received Â£1.9 million in 2011.
  • Cedars Park Bees – In recent years, the Friends Group set up a Pollination Sanctuary (apiary) to help revive bee populations. Fresh honey and Beekeeping Experiences are available. To enquire, please contact chair@cedarspark.info.
  • Men's Shed – We now operate a Men's Shed in the park. Please contact us for more information.
  • Tree Identification Project – Volunteers from our Friends Group identified the species of over 600 trees in Cedars Park and listed them to create a formal arboretum. You can find this information on the Trees page.
