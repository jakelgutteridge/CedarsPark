Friends of Cedars Park

The Friends of Cedars Park are a voluntary group established in 2005 with the purpose of maintaining and improving Cedars Park in complement to the work of Broxbourne Borough Council. The group has over 400 registered members and is led by a committee of 6 people.

The Friends Group carries out a number of essential activities in the park, including maintaining flowerbeds; organising public events; supporting the health of our community; and much more.

For more information, please visit us on Facebook or, if you wish to join the group, please contact chair@cedarspark.info.
